CORONAVIRUS

Coronavirus is a family of virus that cause diseases in mammals and birds. The diseases that are caused can be mild or fatal. Mild illness can cause common cold and fatal illness can cause COVID-19. It was first identified in Wuhan, Hubei, China. The first confirmed case was identified on 17 November, 2019 in Hubei, China. The virus is usually spared between people during close contact, most often via small droplets produced by coughing, sneezing and talking. Humans are capable of spreading the virus to other animals also.

Common symptoms include fever, cough, fatigue, shortness of breath and loss of smell and taste. Some rare symptoms are coughing up sputum and muscle aches and pains.

Fever is the most common symptom of COVID-19, but is highly variable in severity and presentation. In one study only 44% people had fever when the showed up at the hospital, while 89% went on to develop fever at some point during their isolation.

Precautions

  • The most important precaution is to have our distance(at least 1 meter) with others, specially the ones with the symptoms.
  • Frequently wash your hands for a minimum of 20 seconds with a soap or an alcohol-based sanitizer.
  • If you have any of the symptoms go far a medical checkup immediately or self isolate yourself for a minimum of 14 days.
